Privacy First: Receipt Scanner is designed with your privacy as the top priority. All your data is stored locally on your device. We do not sell, share, or rent your personal information to third parties.

1. Information We Collect

Receipt Scanner collects and processes the following information:

  • Receipt Images: Photos you capture or upload of your receipts
  • Extracted Receipt Data: Store names, dates, item names, prices, and purchase information extracted from your receipts
  • Inventory Data: Item tracking and purchase history you create within the app
  • Shopping Lists: Items you add to your shopping lists
  • App Usage Data: Information about how you use the app (stored locally only)

2. How We Store Your Data

Local Storage (Primary): All your receipt images, extracted data, inventory, and shopping lists are stored locally on your device using AsyncStorage. This data never leaves your device unless you use optional cloud processing features.

3. Cloud Processing (Optional)

Receipt Scanner offers two OCR (Optical Character Recognition) modes:

Local OCR (Default - Offline)

  • Processes receipts entirely on your device using Tesseract.js
  • No internet connection required
  • Images and data never leave your device
  • 100% private and secure

Cloud AI OCR (Optional - Online)

  • Uses OpenAI's GPT-4 Vision for superior accuracy
  • Receipt images are sent to OpenAI's servers via encrypted HTTPS connection
  • OpenAI processes the image and returns extracted text data
  • Data Retention: OpenAI retains data for 30 days for abuse monitoring, then permanently deletes it
  • No personally identifiable information (PII) is sent with images
  • You can disable cloud processing anytime in Privacy Settings

When you enable cloud processing, you consent to your receipt images being sent to OpenAI for OCR analysis. You can revoke this consent anytime in the app's Privacy Settings.

4. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information solely to:

  • Extract purchase information from receipt images using OCR
  • Organize and categorize your receipts
  • Track your inventory and purchase history
  • Generate spending analytics and reports
  • Provide shopping list functionality
  • Improve app functionality and user experience
Important: We do NOT use your data for advertising, marketing, or any purpose other than providing app functionality.

5. Data Sharing and Third Parties

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties.

The only third-party service that may process your data is:

  • OpenAI (Optional): Only when you use cloud OCR processing. OpenAI processes receipt images for text extraction and retains data for 30 days for abuse monitoring before permanent deletion. See OpenAI's privacy policy at: https://openai.com/privacy

6. Data Security

We take data security seriously:

  • All data stored locally on your device is protected by your device's security features (passcode, biometric authentication)
  • Cloud processing uses encrypted HTTPS connections (TLS 1.2 or higher)
  • No data is stored on our servers - everything remains on your device
  • Receipt images in cloud processing are sent securely and deleted after 30 days

7. Your Privacy Rights

You have complete control over your data:

Right to Access

All your data is stored locally on your device and accessible anytime within the app.

Right to Export

Export all your receipt data, inventory, and shopping lists in JSON format via the Privacy Settings screen.

Right to Delete

Permanently delete all your data from your device via the Privacy Settings screen. This action cannot be undone.

Right to Revoke Consent

Disable cloud processing anytime in Privacy Settings. The app will automatically switch to local OCR mode.

Right to Object

You can object to cloud processing by disabling it in settings or simply using the app in offline mode.
